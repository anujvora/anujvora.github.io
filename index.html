Anuj S. Vora

 

Postdoctoral researcher
Delft Center for Systems and Control
TU Delft

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Delft Center for Systems and Control group at the ME department at TU Delft. I am working with Prof. Peyman Mohajerin Esfahani.

In 2022, I graduated with a Ph.D. in Systems and Control Engineering from Indian Insitute of Technology, Bombay. I was advised by Prof. Ankur A. Kulkarni in Systems and Control Engineering Department at IIT Bombay. I was also a postdoctoral researcher with Prof. Ankur for a short while. Before my doctorate, I obtained my B.Tech and M.Tech in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Madras in 2016.

I have broad interests in solving problems related to decision-making in uncertain environment, with incomplete information and in the presence of competing agents. I model and solve problems using mathematical tools such as optimization algorithms, heuristics in online learning paradigms, probability and game theory. I am interested in applying theory to develop novel techniques to solve practical problems.
